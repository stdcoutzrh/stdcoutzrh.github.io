I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at East China Normal University (ECNU), advised by Prof. Lizhuang Ma in the Digital Media & Computer Vision Laboratory (DMCV). I am also co-supervised by Prof. Xin Tan.

Prior to starting my Ph.D. studies, I completed my master’s degree under the supervision of Prof. Qian Zhang at ECNU.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Github

Research

  • My research interests involve computer vision and deep learning.
  • Currently, I focus on foundation models for 3D vision, including spatial reasoning, 3D reconstruction, and 3D Gaussian Splatting.
  • Previously, I also worked on 2D image segmentation, especially for remote sensing images.
